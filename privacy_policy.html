Privacy Policy

At Khé, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your data.



Information We Store:

  • User Information & Post Information: This includes your name, username, email, and any content you upload to the app.
  • User Connections: We store data on how you interact with other users within the app.
  • Device Data: We collect information about your device, such as operating system and software version, to optimize our services.


How We Use Your Data:

  • User Information: We use your name, username, and email for account access and communication purposes.
  • Media Scanning: Uploaded media may be scanned to ensure compliance with our Terms of Use.
  • Connection Analysis: Understanding user interactions helps us improve features and address issues.
  • Device Information: We use device data to optimize app performance and enhance security.


How Your Data Is Shared:

  • Uploaded Content: Content you upload may be shared with other users as per app functionality.
  • Device Information: We may share device analytics with third-party companies to improve our services.


GDPR Compliance:

  • Under the EU's General Data Protection Regulation, you have rights regarding your data, including access, rectification, portability, and erasure.
  • You can unsubscribe from marketing communications and object to data processing based on legitimate interests.

Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your data in accordance with applicable regulations.



Data Retention and Account Deletion

We understand the importance of your privacy and offer you the option to delete your account data. Upon account deletion, all associated data will be permanently removed, including:

  • Username, email, and personal information
  • Media content or posts
